For The Children To Laugh

Our association is an association established to reach children and their families who live in a low-income family environment, work on the street due to unfavorable conditions, have interrupted or partially continue their education and improve their conditions.

25 January 2021

Falling Snows Winter Action

We were together with our friends in need, sometimes in an abandoned building on the street, sometimes in slums under the bridge, sometimes in slums where the address of poor income is known and lived in a communal way.

25 January 2021

Profession For Young People

We have provided the invisible youth of the dark streets with a warm bed, unused clothes, and a life free from matter and crime. While doing these, we proved together what young people, who are constantly stigmatized due to their bad habits and where they live, can do when given the opportunity.

25 January 2021
